The Problem

Texts run the transaction.
Screenshots fail the dispute.

Real estate agents conduct business across dozens of simultaneous text conversations — buyers, sellers, inspectors, lenders, TCs, co-op agents. When disputes arise, there is no reliable way to reconstruct the complete communication record.

"I knew exactly what was said. I just couldn't prove it."

Screenshots can be cropped, altered, and selectively presented. They carry no carrier authentication, no chain of custody, and no professional credibility in a DRE complaint or legal proceeding. Agents are exposed — and most don't realize it until it's too late.

📸 Screenshots Are Not Evidence

Out of order. No carrier authentication. Easy to claim they were altered or incomplete. Not credible in professional or legal settings.

💬 Conversations Are Scattered

One transaction involves dozens of separate threads across multiple parties. No existing tool consolidates them into a single authenticated record.

⚖️ The Exposure Is Real

DRE complaints, E&O claims, commission disputes. The documentation problem costs agents thousands — and there has been no professional solution. Until now.

The Solution

RecallText™ — DocuSign for conversations.

A transaction-based communication documentation system. Agents organize conversations by deal, export a certified multi-party record, and store it privately — all on their own device.

📁

Transaction-Based Organization

Communications organized by deal — not by contact. Multiple conversations across all parties become one certified record. No other SMS tool does this.

🔐

Zero-Server Architecture

All processing happens on the user's device. Nothing stored on external servers. RecallText processes, produces, and disappears — leaving only the certified record with the agent.

📄

Certified PDF — The Only Output

PDF only by design. Non-editable. Carrier-authenticated sender IDs. SHA-256 hash verification. Chain of custody certification. Built for disputes.

📍

Mark Beginning of Record

Agents designate the exact message where the transaction record starts. Personal messages before that point excluded — without being deleted from the device.

🎙️

Voicemail and Call Log Integration

Voicemail transcriptions and native call log entries included in the certified record. Because disputes aren't just about texts.

🏠

Built for Real Estate First

Industry templates pre-populate party roles: buyer, seller, inspector, TC, lender, co-op agent. The workflow mirrors how agents actually work transactions.

How It Works

From transaction to certified record in minutes.

1

Open and Index

App opens and reads all messages from the device's native database. No manual import. No server connection. Everything already there.

2

Create Transaction

Name the deal. Select a category. Industry templates pre-populate party roles automatically.

3

Add and Scope

Select multiple conversations simultaneously. Set the exact start point. Add voicemails, call logs, and contemporaneous notes.

4

Export and Store

One certified PDF. All parties. All conversations. SHA-256 verified. Stored in the agent's personal files only.
